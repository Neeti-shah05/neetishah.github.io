Welcome to My Website

Hi, I’m Neeti Shah, a creative professional passionate about data science, art, technology, and storytelling. I hold a double major in IT and English from the University of Massachusetts, Boston, where I developed a unique ability to merge creativity with technology.

As the president of the UMass Boston Art Club, I honed my leadership and organizational skills while fostering a creative community. My passion for technology and writing led me to work at the UMass Writing Center, where I assisted students, presented at conferences, and conducted research. Beyond academics, I organized volunteer trips through Beacon Voyages for Service, fostering collaboration and leadership.

When I’m not coding or painting, I enjoy exploring new cuisines, reading books that expand my horizons, and traveling to scenic destinations. Feel free to explore my journey, connect with me, and let’s create something extraordinary together!
